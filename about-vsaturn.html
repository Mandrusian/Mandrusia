VSaturn

Version 2.2

Overview

VSaturn is your all-in-one media downloader, designed for seamless content acquisition from popular online platforms. It simplifies the process of downloading videos and audio from YouTube, TikTok, Soundcloud, and Instagram.

Features

  • Download from YouTube, TikTok, Soundcloud, Instagram.
  • Sleek, modern GUI with a "liquid glass" aesthetic.
  • Intuitive multi-page navigation.
  • Built with Python and PyQt6.
